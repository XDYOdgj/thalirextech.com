OTOSTAR Waterproof Pillow Inserts

Enhance your outdoor or indoor space with our durable and stylish waterproof pillow inserts. Designed to withstand moisture and daily wear, these inserts are perfect for any living area or outdoor setting.

Each waterproof pillow insert comes with a premium polyester cover that retains its shape and has a soft, elegant texture. The siliconized fiber filling offers superior plumpness and support, making it comfortable for use as a headrest or lumbar cushion.

These inserts are ideal for use with our waterproof pillow cases, creating a double barrier against water. Whether you're lounging on a patio, relaxing in your garden, or decorates your furniture, these inserts add both comfort and style.

With an invisible zipper, you can easily stuff and unstuff your inserts, ensuring a hassle-free experience. The vacuum packing makes it simple to store, and when unfurled, they expand to their full size in just 2-7 days, providing quick and easy use whenever needed.
