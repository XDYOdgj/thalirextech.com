CrystalClear AlgaeOff: The Trusted Algae Remover for Clean Ponds

CrystalClear AlgaeOff is a powerful and safe algaecide designed to control and eliminate string algae in ponds, water gardens, and outdoor water features. Using advanced oxygen technology, it safely and effectively targets stubborn algae growth in hard-to-reach areas like rocks, waterfalls, and stream beds. This EPA registered product not only removes algae mats but also helps eliminate unpleasant odors and green stains, leaving your water garden looking and feeling fresh.

Why Choose CrystalClear AlgaeOff?

  • EPA Registered Algaecide: Formulated for safe and effective use in aquatic environments.
  • Quick and Efficient: Kills string algae rapidly on contact, even in the toughest spots.
  • Odor and Stain Eliminator: Removes foul smells and green stains, restoring clarity to your water.
  • Pet and Wildlife Safe: No harmful copper content ensures safety for animals, birds, and pets when used as directed.
  • Easy Application: The granular formula is simple to use and ideal for large ponds and lakes.

Perfect for pond clean-outs, CrystalClear AlgaeOff offers a fast, efficient, and user-friendly solution to maintain a clean and healthy aquatic environment. Whether you have a small backyard pond with koi or a larger natural water body, this product is your go-to option for algae control. For best results, apply it directly to affected areas and consider turning off the water pump during treatment. Experience the difference of a safe, eco-friendly algaecide that keeps your water clear and your garden beautiful.
