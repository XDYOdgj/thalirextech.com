AquaMiracle Submersible Pond Pump

Enhance your aquatic environment with the AquaMiracle submersible pond pump, engineered for efficiency, durability, and performance. Whether you're tending to a pond, setting up an aquarium, or managing a hydroponic system, this pump delivers seamless water circulation that keeps your setup thriving.

Key Features

  • Energy-efficient design with 70Watt power consumption
  • Max flow rate of 1200GPH (4500L/H) and 10ft (3m) lift capability
  • IP68 sealed motor for safety and durability
  • Adjustable flow rate via front housing regulator
  • Versatile use in ponds, fountains, aquariums, and hydroponics

Why Choose This Pump?

Crafted with a permanent magnet rotor and a specially designed impeller, the AquaMiracle pump provides strong water flow while maintaining low energy consumption. Its IP68 rating ensures it can withstand harsh underwater conditions, making it a reliable choice for both freshwater and marine environments.

The removable front inlet screen simplifies installation and maintenance, saving you time and effort. Plus, the built-in handle makes it easy to move the pump between locations, whether you're setting up a new water feature or adjusting your current system. This pump is built to be a long-term solution for all your aquatic and irrigation needs, blending performance with convenience.

Optimal for Every Water Application

Whether you're working on a water garden, a decorative pond, or a home hydroponic setup, the AquaMiracle pump is designed to adapt. Its consistent flow and adjustable settings mean you can tailor it to suit your specific project requirements. A great choice for both hobbyists and professionals, this pump ensures clean and efficient water movement with minimal hassle.
