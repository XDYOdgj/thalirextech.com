Bloom Buster - Fast Acting Algaecide for Clear Water in Ponds & Fountains

Keep your pond or fountain looking pristine with Bloom Buster, the premier solution for algae control. Designed for pond owners who want beautiful, healthy water without the hassle.

Why Choose Bloom Buster?

Bloom Buster is a powerful, fast-acting algaecide that's specifically formulated to tackle algae problems in outdoor ponds and fountains. It's EPA certified, ensuring it's safe for aquatic life like koi and other fish, as well as plants, wildlife, and pets. This makes it an ideal choice for natural water features where maintaining a balanced ecosystem is important.

What It Eliminates?

Whether it's green water blooms, filamentous algae on plants, floating mats, black beard algae, or blanket weed, Bloom Buster is up to the task. With its broad-spectrum formula, it addresses a wide range of freshwater algae, leaving your pond crystal clear and visually appealing. It also reduces the need for frequent manual cleaning, helping you save time and effort.

Key Features

  • ✅ EPA Registered Algaecide for Safe Use with Fish and Plants
  • ✅ Effective Against Green, Blue-Green, and String Algae
  • ✅ Treats Up to 23,040 Gallons of Water per Bottle
  • ✅ Ideal for Use in Fountains and Small Ponds
  • ✅ Easy to Apply with Dosage Instructions Provided

Ideal for Beginners and Experts

Whether you're maintaining a small ornamental pond or a larger fountain system, Bloom Buster is designed to simplify the process. It's compatible with pond clarifiers and dyes, making it a versatile option for any pond care routine. Plus, the clear application instructions mean you can get results with confidence—without the guesswork.

Perfect for Outdoor Pond Maintenance

Bloom Buster is the best choice for those looking to preserve the beauty of their outdoor water features. It's a trusted solution that helps maintain water clarity and health while being gentle on the environment. Say goodbye to murky water and hello to a cleaner, more inviting outdoor space.
