Enhance Your Water Features with Style and Function

The HGSERVU Pond Lights are a versatile and eye-catching addition to any water garden, pond, or fountain. With their sleek, waterproof design and vibrant color-changing capabilities, these LED submersible lights bring a magical touch to your outdoor space. Featuring 13 stable RGB color combinations, you can customize the ambiance to match your mood or occasion, whether it's a serene evening or a special celebration.

These lights are designed for both performance and ease of use. The included remote control lets you manage the lights from up to 49 feet away, and the timer function gives you the flexibility to set them for 2, 4, or 6 hours. Secure installation is made simple with 4 suction cups that stick firmly to smooth glass surfaces, or you can weigh them down with stones for stability in natural water settings.

Crafted from durable heavy-duty ABS plastic, the HGSERVU Pond Lights are built to last and withstand the elements. Perfect for shallow pools, waterfalls, or even aquariums, they deliver a stunning and customizable underwater light display without compromising on safety or longevity.
