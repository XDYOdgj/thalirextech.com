Elevate Your Outdoor Space with the AquaMiracle All-in-One Fountain Kit

Transform your pond or koi pond into a captivating outdoor feature with this advanced, all-in-one fountain system. Designed for ease of use and maximum beauty, it combines powerful water circulation, elegant water displays, and smart illumination in one sleek package.

Powerful Performance for Healthy Water

The AquaMiracle All-in-One Fountain Kit features a robust 850GPH pump that ensures efficient water movement and oxygenation, keeping your pond clean and healthy. Whether you have a small water garden or a larger koi pond, this system is built to deliver consistent results.

Customizable Water Displays

With three distinct spray patterns, you can enhance your pond's visual appeal with options like a gentle bell-shaped spray, a bold volcano-style cascade, or a soft daisy-like flow. This flexibility allows you to create a personal touch that complements your outdoor decor.

Smart LED Lighting for Nighttime Magic

Enjoy a mesmerizing nighttime display with the integrated smart LED lights that activate automatically at dusk. These lights come with color cycling options, turning your pond into a glowing centerpiece that enhances the ambiance of your garden.

Advanced Filtration for Clear Water

The kit includes a 4-layer filtration system that works with coarse and fine sponges, ceramic rings, and an 8W UV light to remove debris, eliminate green water algae, and support the growth of beneficial bacteria. This ensures your pond stays crystal clear all year round.

Convenient and Versatile Design

Its dual function design allows the secondary outlet to be used for streams or other water features, making it a versatile choice for both decorative and functional ponds. Everything you need is included, so installation and maintenance are simple and hassle-free.

Perfect for Any Outdoor Pond

Whether you're looking to upgrade your backyard water garden or enhance the look of a koi pond, the AquaMiracle Fountain Kit is the ideal solution. It's designed for ponds up to 750 gallons and provides a seamless blend of utility and beauty.

Experience the beauty of a well-maintained pond with the AquaMiracle All-in-One Fountain Kit. It's your gateway to a serene and enchanting outdoor space.
